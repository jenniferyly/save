About

Circle K International (CKI) is a student led community service organization with clubs all over the world. This is the website made for the Circle K International campus club at the University of California, Davis. While the theme of the club and thus the theme of the website changes every year, the 2018-2019 template has persisted up through and including the current year's (2020) design.

History

During my first term as the webmaster and creative director of the DavisCKI website, I was mainly concerned about picking up the slack my predecessor had left behind. Their 2016-2017 design (not shown) was fairly minimalistic and clean to look at, however, it lacked a lot the necessary working components that the website was meant to be there for. The website is a potential first impression for new and prospective members to understand what the organization does while also providing older, returning members of the club somewhere to quickly get new information and resources. My main concern was to make the homepage a quick peek at what's going on that most people would likely only ever need to see the home page but the extra bits of information are just as quickly accessible for those who want it.

Context

Every year, clubs in Circle K International choose a theme for their club to design unique graphics and other fun elements around. The theme for the UCDavis club in the 2018-2019 term was Greek Mythology.

2018 Initial Revamp

While I do not have the 2017-2018 website archived, I do have the initial revamp the website was undergoing for the 2018-2019 year. Added and retained from my 2017-2018 design, the Upcoming Events were visible on the homepage first thing after our welcome banner. The club's total hours were also added as something to keep returning members motivated and allow them to visibly see that the time they put into the club really do add up.

2018-2019 Finalized Revamp

I had attempted to fix what I originally had on the website from my previous term's work, however, in the end I decided to start from scratch and look at existing wix templates for inspiration. After playing around with the templates, I was able to settle on this design. I still kept many of the elements previously seen but designed it in a more centered view with portions that extend to the edges of the screen to also break up the information into more digestible chunks.

1st Place Outstanding Club Website

The Outstanding Club Website award is given to the top 3 club websites submitted to the California-Nevada-Hawaii (CNH) District of Circle K International. The website is submitted for ongoing judging in early October and the award is handed out in March the next year. The website must meet a list of criteria as well be submitted with writtten answers to explain certain aspects of the website. Some criteria include: adherring to the Circle K Internation brand guide, including links to other associated club websites if available, incorporating your club's chosen theme, etc. It is also judged upon organization, ease of use, and aesthetic. The Circle K International club at the University of California, Davis (DavisCKI) won the 1st place for this award for the 2018-2019 term.
