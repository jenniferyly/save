Obey Me Fanart

Fanart I made of my favorite character from the mobile game Obey Me! by NTT Solmare Corp. during their in-game summer festival event. The main characters received new yukata outfits to go with the theme, however, this is a side character and he didn't receive such a make over. I designed my own yukata around the yukata designed in the game and created some mock illustrations in the game's style.
